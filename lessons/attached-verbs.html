Attached Pronouns with Verbs

ضَمَائِر مُتَّصِلَة مَعَ الْأَفْعَال

When attached to a verb, the pronoun acts as the object. The verb precedes the pronoun; in translation, the verb comes first, then the pronoun (object).

Example: خَلَقَ (He created)

Pronoun suffix (object)

PersonSingularDualPlural
3rd Masc.خَلَقَهُ (He created him)خَلَقَهُمَاخَلَقَهُمْ
3rd Fem.خَلَقَهَا (He created her)خَلَقَهُمَاخَلَقَهُنَّ
2nd Masc.خَلَقَكَ (He created you)خَلَقَكُمَاخَلَقَكُمْ
2nd Fem.خَلَقَكِخَلَقَكُمَاخَلَقَكُنَّ
1stخَلَقَنِي (He created me)—خَلَقَنَا (He created us)
Rule: For 1st person singular with verbs, use ـنِي (nī), e.g. خَلَقَنِي (He created me).

With Particles (e.g. لَ)

لَهُ — to him (2:131)
فَلَهُمْ — for them (2:62)
قَبْلِكُمْ — before you (2:21)

Quranic Examples

خَلَقَكُمْ — (He) created you (2:21)
فَأَزَلَّهُمَا — made both of them slip (2:36)
وَتَرَكَهُمْ — and left them (2:17)
