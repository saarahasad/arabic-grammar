Vocabulary — Body Parts

مُفْرَدَاتُ الْجَسَد

Memorization list: Arabic nouns for parts of the body (and closely related words), with singular (مُفْرَد) and plural (جَمْع). For plural patterns, see Noun → Number → جَمْع.
