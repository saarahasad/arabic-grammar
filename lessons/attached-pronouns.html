Attached Pronouns

ضَمَائِر مُتَّصِلَة

Attached pronouns only appear attached to another word — a noun, verb, or particle.

Table of Attached Pronouns (Suffixes)

PersonSingularDualPlural
3rd Masculine
—ـهُ (him/his)ـهُمَاـهُمْ
3rd Feminine
—ـهَا (her)ـهُمَاـهُنَّ
2nd Masculine
—ـكَ (you/your)ـكُمَاـكُمْ
2nd Feminine
—ـكِـكُمَاـكُنَّ
1st Person
—ـي / ـنِي (me/my)—ـنَا (us/our)

Memorization Rules

Rule 1: Third-person pronouns begin with هـ. Rule 2: Dual pronouns end with مَا. Rule 3: Masculine plural end with مْ. Rule 4: Feminine plural end with نَّ. Rule 5: Second-person begin with كـ.

See With Nouns and With Verbs for how these attach in practice.
