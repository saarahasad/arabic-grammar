Nūn — secondary sign of Raf'

النُّونُ

For the five verbs (الْأَفْعَالُ الْخَمْسَةُ), the sign of Raf' is ثُبُوتُ النُّونِ — the nūn remains pronounced at the end.

يَذْهَبُونَYadhhabūna
